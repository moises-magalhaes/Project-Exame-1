Who are we?

SpaceX (Space Exploration Technologies Corporation) is an American company for aeroespacial activities. We were founded in 2002 with the plan of flying commercially to space. We are the pioneer in launching and returning a spacecraft from and back to Earth. We are doing it! The Dragon spacecraft is the first commercial spacecraft that has delivered cargo to and from the International Space Station. And the next step is to bring people ;). We developed a space rocket that greatly reduces the costs of launching it to orbit. That was possible by returning the craft intact, recovering, maintaining and reusing it. Before, the craft flew only, that caused the costs to be very high. We follow the commercial model of flying. To exemplify it, Our spacecraft Falcon 9 costs similar to a normal airplane, and as the airplane, the plan is to fly thousands of flights. That way we can reduce extremely the costs of spaceflight, bringing new opportunities for what the future can give us.
